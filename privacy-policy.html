Privacy Policy

HybridPulse — Last updated: February 20, 2026

Overview

HybridPulse is a workout tracker designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal data. All information you enter stays on your device.

Data Storage

All workout logs, routines, body measurements, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. No data is sent to external servers.

Apple HealthKit

HybridPulse can optionally integrate with Apple Health to:

  • Read body metrics (weight, heart rate, heart rate variability)
  • Write completed workouts

This access is entirely optional and controlled by you through iOS Settings. Health data is never sent outside your device.

iCloud Sync

If you enable iCloud, your data may sync between your devices through Apple's private CloudKit container. Apple manages the encryption and transmission of this data. We have no access to your iCloud data.

Progress Photos

Progress photos you take or import are stored locally on your device only. They are not uploaded to any server.

Notifications

HybridPulse may send local notifications for workout reminders and rest timers. These are generated entirely on your device. There is no push notification server.

Analytics, Advertising & Third-Party SDKs

HybridPulse contains:

  • No analytics or tracking frameworks
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No third-party data collection of any kind

Children's Privacy

HybridPulse does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
