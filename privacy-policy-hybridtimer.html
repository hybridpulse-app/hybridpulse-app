Privacy Policy

HybridTimer — Last updated: February 27, 2026

Overview

HybridTimer is a workout interval timer designed with privacy at its core. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

Data Storage

All workout history, presets, and settings are stored locally on your device. No data is ever sent to external servers or third parties.

Live Activities

HybridTimer uses iOS Live Activities to display your active timer on the Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. This feature is powered entirely by your device. No timer data is transmitted anywhere.

Analytics, Advertising & Third-Party SDKs

HybridTimer contains:

  • No analytics or tracking frameworks
  • No advertising SDKs
  • No third-party data collection of any kind

Children's Privacy

HybridTimer does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be posted on this page with a revised date.
